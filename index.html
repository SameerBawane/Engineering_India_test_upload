About Us

Engineering India is a central club dedicated to leveraging the collective power of engineers and technology enthusiasts for the betterment of society. Engineering India serves as a platform for collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking among like-minded individuals who share a common goal. By harnessing the passion and talents of its members, Engineering India is driving positive change, making a lasting impact, and contributing to the betterment of society on both local and national scales.

Our Events

Engineering India has hosted various impactful events: “Abhyudaya” at VNIT Nagpur showcased student talents, while “Shram-Parihar” united coordinators from multiple institutions. “Vaccine War” celebrated scientific advances, and Makar Sankranti featured drama to raise awareness about Nylon Manjha. “Rangeettalim” involved slum visits by YCCE and Raisoni coordinators, and “Manthan” by YCCE offered an engaging online lecture series. These events highlight our dedication to innovation, community service, and education.
